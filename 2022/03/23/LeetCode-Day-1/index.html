Best Sightseeing Pair

You are given an integer array values where values[i] represents the value of the sightseeing spot. Two sightseeing spots i and j have a distance j - i between them.

The score of a pair (i < j) of sightseeing spots is values[i] + values[j] + i - j: the sum of the values of the sightseeing spots, minus the distance between them.

Return the maximum score of a pair of sightseeing spots .

values[i] + values[j] + i - j is also equal to values[i] + i + values[j]-j

As a result. this question also can be seem as find the most values[i] + i and values[j]-j.

When scanning every elemnet in the values, choose most values[i] + i and values[j]-j.

To be noted, the max is decreasing as we i increasing;

Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock II

You are given an integer array prices where prices[i] is the price of a given stock on the i<sup>th</sup> day.

On each day, you may decide to buy and/or sell the stock. You can only hold at most one share of the stock at any time. However, you can buy it then immediately sell it on the same day .

Find and return the maximum profit you can achieve .

Input: prices = [7,1,5,3,6,4]
Output: 7
Explanation: Buy on day 2 (price = 1) and sell on day 3 (price = 5), profit = 5-1 = 4.
Then buy on day 4 (price = 3) and sell on day 5 (price = 6), profit = 6-3 = 3.
Total profit is 4 + 3 = 7.

Explannation:

When meeting the lower price, we buy it and sell it when meet the higher price.
